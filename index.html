Important Safety Disclaimer

Coding your vehicle comes with inherent risks. Please read and understand the following before proceeding:

  • Warranty: Modifying your vehicle's software may void parts of your manufacturer's warranty.
  • Backups: Bimmercode creates backups. Ensure you have one before making changes.
  • Vehicle Health: Only code with a stable power supply (battery tender recommended).
  • Responsibility: You are solely responsible for any changes made. Expert Mode requires extra caution. If unsure, do not change the setting.
